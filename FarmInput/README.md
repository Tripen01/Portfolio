# FarmInput: Accessible Privacy Policy Development

## About This Project
This project involved the creation of a user-centric Privacy Policy for **FarmInput**, a mobile application that empowers farmers to track market prices for inputs (seeds, fertilizers, etc.) and collaborate with peers. The primary goal was to ensure that the policy was transparent and easily understood by the farming community.

## Extended Description
Because FarmInput handles sensitive data regarding farm expenses and peer-to-peer collaborations, the Privacy Policy was designed to build trust. Working alongside a collaborative team, I focused on translating complex data-sharing protocols into plain language that clearly explains how a farmer’s financial and logistical information is protected.

### Key Focus Areas:
* **Plain Language Writing:** Stripping away "legalese" to explain data protection in terms relevant to the agricultural sector.
* **Information Hierarchy:** Organizing the policy so farmers can quickly find answers to "Who sees my price data?" and "How is my collaboration data stored?"
* **Data Transparency:** Clearly outlining the boundaries of data sharing to encourage confident use of the app’s collaborative features.
* **User Advocacy:** Designing the documentation to respect the user's right to understand how their business insights are handled.
